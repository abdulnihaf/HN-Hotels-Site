Leakage Detection Framework
Where money disappears

1. Portion Control Drift

Signal: Actual food cost > theoretical by >3%
Fix: Recipe cards with gram weights, calibrated scoops, spot checks

2. Theft & Pilferage

Signal: Shrinkage >3%, unexplained stock-outs
Fix: CCTV, daily counts on high-value items (chicken, oil, cheese), POS void tracking

3. Aggregator Reconciliation Gaps

Signal: Payout < expected by >2% — 72% of restaurants affected
Fix: Weekly line-by-line reconciliation, dispute unverified refunds within 7 days

4. Energy Waste

Signal: Utility bills >5% of revenue
Fix: Equipment checklists, timer switches, energy audit

5. Over-Ordering / Spoilage

Signal: Spoilage >3%, dead stock accumulation
Fix: Par level system, purchase orders tied to sales forecast, FIFO

6. Discount Leakage

Signal: Unexplained margin erosion on specific items
Fix: Discount approval workflow, weekly promo cost analysis

7. Cash Leakage

Signal: Cash register short by >Rs 100/day
Fix: Daily cash reconciliation, surprise audits, push digital payments

Once the structure is in place and data flows in, here is where AI becomes 100x more efficient:

Automatic Anomaly Detection

Flag if food cost % deviates >2% from 7-day average. Flag if aggregator order count mismatches POS by >2 orders. Flag if actual vs theoretical food cost gap >3%.

"Chicken cost up 15% this week — supplier issue or waste?"
Predictive Intelligence

Demand forecasting by day of week, weather, festivals. Supplier price prediction — buy when cheap. Labor scheduling optimized to expected demand.

"Tomorrow is rainy + Thursday = 40% fewer dine-in, increase delivery prep"
Automated Reconciliation

Match every aggregator order to POS transaction. Auto-calculate expected vs actual payout. Auto-flag refund deductions for dispute.

"Zomato payout Rs 3,200 less than expected — 4 disputed refunds found"
Menu Intelligence

Real-time contribution margin per item. Auto-classify into Stars/Plowhorses/Puzzles/Dogs. Suggest price adjustments based on demand elasticity.
