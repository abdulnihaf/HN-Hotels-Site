Important technical truth:

We can optimize for Google Maps behavior, but we cannot force a Search campaign to serve only inside Google Maps. The correct Maps-focused implementation is: accurate GBP link, location asset, exact radius/presence targeting, local-intent keywords, night-only schedule, and click-type reporting for location detail, directions and mobile calls.

Also, if GBP hours do not show the outlet open during 22:00-03:00, ad copy must not claim "open late" until the business hours/special hours are accurate.
