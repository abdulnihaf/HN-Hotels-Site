About Us

Heritage Hospitality, Modern Excellence

HN Hotels Private Limited is a Bangalore-based hospitality company that operates a portfolio of heritage food and beverage brands. Incorporated in December 2023, the company was established to formalize and scale the operations of family-owned hospitality ventures with deep roots in Karnataka's culinary heritage.

Our flagship property, Hamza Hotel, has been serving Bangalore since 1918 â€” making it one of the oldest continuously operating restaurants in the city. Today, HN Hotels operates multiple brands including Hamza Express (QSR Biryani) and Nawabi Chai House (Irani Chai Cafe), each carrying forward the legacy of authentic, time-tested recipes.

As a registered Micro Enterprise under the MSME Act, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality while embracing modern operational practices and technology to serve our customers better.
