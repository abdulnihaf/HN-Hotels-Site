How This Engine Works

Layered architecture. Eight data layers feed a signal engine that scores every Nifty-500 stock daily. Money rotates only when the signal score-delta justifies it.

  1. Layer 1 — Price & Volume (10 sources): NSE/BSE bhavcopies, indices, pre-open, intraday, delivery %, GIFT Nifty, 52w extremes, circuits. Live
  2. Layer 2 — Institutional Flow (8 sources): FII/DII cash + derivatives, F&O participant OI, bulk/block deals, F&O ban list, MWPL. Live
  3. Layer 3 — Options & Volatility (5 sources): Nifty/BankNifty/FinNifty/stock option chains + India VIX. Live
  4. Layer 4 — Corporate Intelligence (8 sources): announcements, insider trades, corp actions, board meetings, results, shareholding, pledges. Live
  5. Layer 5 — Macro & Cross-Asset (12 sources): FRED, RBI, GST, MoSPI, PMI, DXY, US10Y, VIX, S&P futures, crude, gold, Asian indices. Live
  6. Layer 6 — News & Sentiment (10 sources): GDELT, ET, MoneyControl, LiveMint, BS, Reuters, BQ, Reddit, Nitter, StockTwits. Live
  7. Layer 7 — Event Calendar (4 sources): Investing.com, NSE earnings, IMD, POSOCO. Live
  8. Layer 8 — Sector & Breadth (4 sources): sector indices, most-active, advance/decline, bond yields. Live

Signal engine (Worker W9) runs nightly at 19:00 IST and assigns each stock a 0-100 composite score across 7 dimensions: trend, flow, options, catalyst, macro, sentiment, breadth.

Money rotation rule: a held position is rotated out only when its composite score drops >15 points below a candidate stock that has score >70 with at least 2 confirming dimensions. Every rotation is logged with full rationale.

Stop-loss & targets are written before entry, never after. Position sizing follows Kelly 0.25× — disciplined, not heroic.

Realistic target: 25-30% annual on the blended ₹10L portfolio. Worst-realistic year: -15%.
