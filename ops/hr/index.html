Remote commands

Reverify is the duplicate-punch guard (seconds). CAMS recognises faces continuously — without reverify, a single pass in front of the camera can log multiple punches. Recommended: 30s (current).

Operational notes

  • Device pushes punches directly to Odoo via paid Odoo Standard Edition API (valid until 31-Jan-2027).
  • This dashboard pulls hr.attendance from Odoo — it does not intercept the device.
  • Ghost punches (< 2h worked) are flagged GHOST and deducted as absent.
  • Single-punch days (IN but no OUT) are flagged and treated as half-day unless shift rule allows.
  • Staff who register and leave: caught by min_daily_hours rule + single-punch detection.
