Local order-volume evidence. Orders, units, AOV, dish family, category, cuisine and veg/combo flags are preserved so we do not flatten demand into one number.

Popularity rank evidence. Avg Price is not treated as exact mode price; rank guides priority and lower-percentile logic protects against high-ticket skew.
