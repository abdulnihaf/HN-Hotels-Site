6. Mandatory Offer Architecture

This is what the old UI failed to convey.
Item Offer

Mutton Biryani premium pull

Row 102 is the cleanest item-level discount candidate. Keep base at Rs425 in the model; test max 15% no-stack if margin proof closes.

Customer sees: premium biryani deal around Rs361.25 effective.
Gate: mutton food cost, packaging, Gold/platform stack, screenshot proof.
Daypart Cart Offer

Zomato lunch conversion badge

The playbook supports a lunch MOV offer such as 20% capped at Rs100 above Rs399, but only when baskets naturally cross or can be guided to cross MOV.

Customer sees: lunch special and offer badge during the weak daypart.
Gate: MOV proof, no overlap with item offer, 14-day kill metric.
Fixed Combo Value

Rice-curry, biryani-kabab, bread-curry baskets

Own HE evidence points toward basket behavior. The better offer may be a fixed combo value with strike-through perception, not another percentage discount.

Customer sees: complete meal value, not cheap individual SKU.
Gate: component portions, cost, packaging, item promise.
Swiggy Offer Lane

Volume-family offer after readiness

Swiggy has the strongest volume data but a weaker execution surface. Its offer packet comes after current price, photo, disabled-row, and combo proof cleanup.

Customer sees: Swiggy-specific offer on already-proven high-volume families.
Gate: current Swiggy menu state and PoC/backend offer path.

8. Corrected Interpretation

What changed after your feedback.

Wrong takeaway

"The intelligence says do not run offers." That was only a safety gate from the earlier UI and does not satisfy the volume objective.

Correct takeaway

"Run offers, but only after the SKU has a role, a price corridor, and a mechanic." The offer is mandatory; the discipline is where and how it is applied.

What this UI now covers

All 35 micro-steps are represented as a visual trail: sources, demand, corridors, menu rows, playbook mechanics, proof gates, P0 roles, price foundation, and offer architecture.

What it does not do

It does not mutate Swiggy/Zomato portals, launch a live offer, or bypass proof. Manual updates remain team-executed.
