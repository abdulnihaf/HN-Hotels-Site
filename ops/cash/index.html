Record Cash Transfer
Pile → Pile only (zero-sum, two-leg). For deposits to bank use the separate "Deposit to Bank" flow.
Deposit Cash to Bank
Records a 1-leg debit on the cash side. The bank credit lands separately when HDFC's email parser sees the deposit.
Reconcile pile (anchor to physical count)
Capture a physical count at a specific timestamp. Going-forward balance = this anchor + sum of events recorded AFTER this moment. Pre-anchor events stay in the trail (visible for audit) but no longer affect the live balance.
Default = now (IST). Adjust if the count happened earlier — every event before this time is "already counted", every event after debits/credits live.
External Capital In
Owner injection or third-party cash entering the system. Notes describing the source are required (≥5 chars).
